    Welcome to the LightChain Network

    Our goal is to become the most user-friendly privacy coin for mobile devices. Unique ideas, dedicated developers, and a strong community are the building blocks of this vision. The Disciple Node ecosystem is our foundation, which brings these ideas to life by creating a fast, efficient, and active network. Join us today.


    Our Values

    Privacy

    We will never compromise privacy or confidentiality for any reason.

    Fairness

    All aspects of this project are fair and will remain fair.

    Community

    This is a community project- by the community, for the community. This is as important to us as privacy.

    Commitment

    Our commitment to making this vision a reality with the help of others will remain uncompromised as we move forward.

    Disciple Nodes

    Our nodes have no collateral requirement and can be operated by anyone. 15% of our block reward is distributed to Disciple Nodes via Sensei, our user-friendly oracle service. For more information, you can find our comprehensive setup guide here.
